Ham

Ham is pork from a leg cut that has been preserved by wet or dry curing, with or without smoking. As a processed meat the term "ham" includes both whole cuts of meat and the ones that have been mechanically formed. Ham is a popular food in many cultures around the world. It is a good source of protein, healthy fats, and minerals. However, it can also be difficult to get uncooked ham.
